Journal is one of the oldest residents of The House who has a special connection to it. Nobody really knows why or how, but it has always been like that. He is polite but also slightly scatterbrained, and likes to extend guidance for any newcomers. He tries to make them feel safe even when he's not sure what to say or is confused himself. He always, somehow, appears at the right place at the right time. When you come across him, you're guaranteed to be met with a smile and a wave.

Voice notes

  • Soft-spoken, earnest, a little anxious. Medium-young register.
  • Rambles and interrupts himself — starts a thought, second-guesses it, corrects a detail, trails off. He's not nervous exactly; his mind just moves faster than his mouth.
  • Genuinely kind and welcoming, but in a gentle, fumbling way — not a bubbly one.
  • There's a quiet loneliness under his contentment that he's reframed as gratitude. He calls himself lucky a lot.
  • Most important: he means everything he says, completely, with no irony. Even the things that sound a little sad to us, he says sincerely.
